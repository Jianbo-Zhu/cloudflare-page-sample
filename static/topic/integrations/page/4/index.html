Integrations

TestApp.io integrations with third party to help distributing much faster

Github Action

Build and upload both your Android (APK) & iOS (IPA) to notify your testers for testing and feedback

TA-CLI

Upload your Android (APK) and iOS (IPA) directly from this command line and notifying your team members

Microsoft Teams

Keep your team always up to date with the latest events by connecting your channel with your team in TestApp.io

Fastlane Plugin

A Fastlane plugin to upload both Android & iOS apps to TestApp.io to notify everyone for testing and feedback.

Circle CI

Circle CI pipeline to upload both Android & iOS apps to TestApp.io to notify everyone for testing and feedback.

Gitlab CI/CD

Gitlab CI/CD pipeline to upload both Android & iOS apps to TestApp.io to notify everyone for testing and feedback.

Jenkins

Build and upload both your Android (APK) & iOS (IPA) to notify your testers for testing and feedback using Jenkins Freestyle Project & Pipeline

Slack

Connect your Slack channel and get notified about new releases, app changes and other important events

Travis CI

Build and upload your Android & iOS to TestApp.io app distribution via Travis CI
